Streamlining Proof of Delivery for Better Business Efficiency

Mobile POD

FlexPod Mobile allows real-time proof of delivery using smartphones, RF handhelds, or truck-mounted devices, streamlining the POD process and improving cash flow. Compatible with all major mobile platforms (iPhone, Android, Windows Mobile, Blackberry).

Voice POD

FlexPod Voice lets hauliers use any phone to report delivery by calling a specific number. The voice application captures the delivery details and generates a POD, ready for billing or further validation processes.

Web POD

FlexPod Web offers an online portal where hauliers can log delivery details. Variances are validated, and the proof of delivery is generated or flagged for resolution. Ready for billing once validated.
